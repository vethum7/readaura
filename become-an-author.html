✨ Why Publish on ReadAura?

ReadAura is a free reading platform where readers from around the world discover new stories every day. We help authors grow their audience, build recognition, and even earn through affiliate links — all for free.

  • Keep 100% ownership of your stories
  • Reach thousands of potential readers
  • Get your own author profile and story pages
  • Earn from your affiliate links and reader support

💰 Earn Through Affiliate Links

Unlike most free reading platforms, ReadAura lets you include your own affiliate links at the end of every chapter. You can earn when readers click your links and buy related products.

  • 🔗 Add Amazon or Audible affiliate links
  • ❤️ Link your Patreon or Ko-fi page
  • 📚 Promote your other books or YouTube channels

You keep 100% of your affiliate earnings — ReadAura takes nothing.

🚀 How to Get Started

  1. Read and accept the Author Agreement (next step).
  2. Prepare your story file (Word, PDF, or text).
  3. Include your affiliate links at the end of each chapter (optional).
  4. Upload your story through the Author Dashboard.

🧾 Affiliate Link & Content Rules

To keep ReadAura a safe and trusted platform, please follow these simple rules:

✅ Allowed

  • Amazon, Audible, or Bookshop.org affiliate links
  • Patreon, Ko-fi, or BuyMeACoffee pages
  • Links to your own website or other books

❌ Not Allowed

  • Adult or explicit content
  • Gambling, crypto, or scam links
  • Spam or misleading advertisements

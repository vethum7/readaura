ReadAura Privacy Policy

At ReadAura, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Information you provide when signing in using Google (such as name and email).
  • Your bookmarks and reading history to improve your experience.
  • Comments you post on books.
  • Usage data like pages visited and interactions within the site.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve ReadAura services.
  • To display your bookmarks and saved content.
  • To manage user comments and community interactions.
  • To monitor site usage and maintain security.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except when required by law or to prevent fraud or abuse.

4. Cookies and Tracking

ReadAura uses minimal cookies and analytics tools to improve your experience. No personally identifiable information is shared with advertisers.

5. Your Rights

  • You can delete your account and associated data at any time.
  • You can request access to any personal data we store about you.
  • You can contact us to correct or update your information.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, you can contact us at: support@readaura.com.

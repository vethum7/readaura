ReadAura Privacy Policy

At ReadAura (readaura.xyz), we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights, especially regarding advertising through Google AdSense.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account info – When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address.
  • Activity data – bookmarks, reading history, comments, and pages you visit on ReadAura.
  • Technical data – IP address, browser type, device info, and cookies (see section 4).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and improve ReadAura services.
  • Remember your bookmarks and preferences.
  • Moderate comments and community interactions.
  • Analyze site usage to enhance user experience.
  • Serve relevant ads via Google AdSense (see below).

3. Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display ads on ReadAura. AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads.
  • Google's DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visits to this site and other sites on the internet.
  • You may opt out of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad & Privacy Policy.
  • Users can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting YourAdChoices or Your Online Choices (EU).

We do not share personally identifiable information with advertisers. However, non-personal information (e.g., browser type, approximate location) may be shared for ad relevance.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use:

  • Essential cookies – for login and site functionality.
  • Analytics cookies – to understand how you use ReadAura (e.g., page views).
  • Advertising cookies – placed by Google AdSense to deliver relevant ads.

You can control or delete cookies via your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site features.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. We may share data only:

  • With trusted service providers (e.g., Google Analytics, AdSense) who process data under strict confidentiality.
  • If required by law or to protect rights, property, or safety.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access – request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction – update inaccurate information.
  • Deletion – request account and data deletion.
  • Opt-out of sale – we do not sell data, so this is not applicable.
  • Withdraw consent – for cookies or personalized ads (manage via browser or ad choices).

To exercise these rights, contact us using the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

ReadAura is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us.

8. Data Retention & Security

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time, after which we remove or anonymize your personal data. We use industry-standard security measures, but no internet transmission is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this page occasionally. The date at the bottom indicates the last revision. Continued use of ReadAura after changes means you accept the updated policy.
